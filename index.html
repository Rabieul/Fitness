⚕️ HS Rules
🔵Shower immediately after every workout — no exceptions
🔵Loose, moisture-wicking gym clothes — nothing tight in problem areas
🔵Fragrance-free soap on affected areas post-workout
🔵Keep a food journal — note which specific foods trigger flares for you
🔵Test nightshades: eliminate tomatoes, eggplant, peppers for 3 weeks and observe
🔵Fatty fish, turmeric, ginger, olive oil — anti-inflammatory eating is your long game
🫘 Kidney Stone Rules
🟡4L water daily — your single most important action. Non-negotiable.
🟡Lemon in water daily — citrate prevents calcium oxalate stone formation
🟡Spread protein across all meals — no single large protein dumps
🟡Low sodium — fast food, salty snacks, processed meats increase stone risk
🟡No high-dose Vitamin C supplements — converts to oxalate
🟡Doctor clearance before starting creatine
